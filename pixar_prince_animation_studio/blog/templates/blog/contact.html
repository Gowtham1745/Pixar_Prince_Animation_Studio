Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What's your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. Simple projects can be completed in 5-7 days, while complex productions may take 4-8 weeks. We provide custom timelines after discussing your specific requirements.

Do you offer package deals?

Yes! We offer flexible pricing packages (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) that can be customized to fit your budget and project needs. Custom packages available upon request.

How many revisions are included?

Revision limits depend on your selected package. Starter includes 2, Professional includes 5, and Enterprise plans offer unlimited revisions. All revisions are made until you're completely satisfied.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all standard formats: MP4, MOV, ProRes, DCP for video; PSD, AI, PNG for graphics; WAV, MP3, AAC for audio. Custom formats available upon request.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Time zone differences are managed through clear communication, scheduled meetings, and detailed project documentation.

What's your payment process?

Typically 50% deposit to start, 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfers, PayPal, and cryptocurrency.
